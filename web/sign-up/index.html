Rules

  • 8 players + Team Captain required.
  • Each university will play every other university.
  • There will be 4 single matches and 2 doubles matches.
  • No player can play more than 1 match in a single day of competition.
  • One university will act as host while the other will be the guest.
  • Whoever wins more matches is the winner.
  • In the event of a tie, whoever scored more sets will win.
  • If scored sets are equal, there will be a tie break set between two players.
